How to Use the Map

1. Hover to Preview

Hover over any state marker to see a quick preview of age requirements and penalties.

2. Click for Details

Click on a state to view complete legal information, requirements, and FAQs.

3. Use Filters

Filter states by age, consent requirements, or strictness level to find specific laws.

4. Color Coding

States are color-coded: Red (18+ only), Yellow (16-17 w/ consent), Green (14-15 w/ consent).
