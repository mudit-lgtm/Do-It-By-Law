Tool FAQ

Is this tool accurate?

Yes! Our tool uses current state laws updated as of November 2025. However, always verify with your local tattoo parlor or state authorities as laws can change.

Do you store my information?

No. All checks are performed in your browser using JavaScript. We don't collect or store any personal information you enter.

What if my state isn't listed?

Phase 1 currently covers Alabama. Phase 2 (coming Week 3-4) will add Alaska, California, Florida, and Georgia. All 50 states will be available by Phase 3.

Can I use this for piercings?

This tool is specifically for tattoo age laws. Piercing laws often differ and will be covered in Phase 5.
