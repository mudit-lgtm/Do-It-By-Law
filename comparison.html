Key Differences Between States

Strictest States

Alabama, Alaska, California, Georgia all require age 18 with NO parental consent exceptions.

Most Lenient

Florida allows tattooing at age 16 with notarized parental consent and presence.

Penalty Variations

Penalties range from Class C misdemeanors (Alabama) to Class B misdemeanors (Alaska).
