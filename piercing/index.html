Understanding Piercing Age Laws

Piercing age laws vary significantly by state and type of piercing. Generally, there are three categories:

👂 Ear Piercing

Most Lenient: Many states allow ear lobe piercing at any age with parental consent. Some states have no specific laws.

Typical Age: Any age with parent present

💍 Body Piercing

More Restrictive: Body piercings (nose, eyebrow, navel) typically require minimum age 14-16 with parental consent.

Typical Age: 14-16 with consent, 18+ without

🔞 Genital Piercing

Most Restrictive: Most states prohibit genital piercing on minors entirely, even with parental consent.

Typical Age: 18+ only (no exceptions)
